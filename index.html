War of the Suits!

Rules for war of the suits! ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Basic rules 1.The board must be six spaces long and three spaces wide (each space is the size of one card) 2.Combos can be disabled 3.2 players 4.Hearts is the suit of player 1 5.Spades is the suit of player 2 6.Diamonds (for player 1) and clubs (for player 2) are the bonus suits which are only placed by combos 7.Bonus suits are put back into their stack if they are defeated 8.Core suites (hearts and spades) are put into the discard pile if they are defeated/discarded ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Card Details [These apply to all of the cards regardless of the suit] Joker{colored or monochrome}:10 health, 10 damage, can move in between a blocked diagonal, is placed in the start of the game Ace:1 health, 1 damage. can be placed as a healing spot 2 of[suit]:2 health, 2 damage 3 of[suit]:3 health, 2 damage, can attack twice 4 of[suit]:4 health, 4 damage 5 of[suit]:5 health, 4 damage, can attack thrice 6 of[suit]:6 health, 6 damage 7 of[suit]:7 health, 2 damage, can attack 4 times 8 of[suit]:8 health, 8 damage 9 of[suit]:9 health, 9 damage, can attack 5 times 10 of[suit]:10 health, 10 damage, can double health once jack of[suit]:11 health, 11 damage, can fortify a queen of its own suit queen of[suit]:12 health, 12 damage, can fortify a king of its own suit king of[suit]:13 health, 13 damage. can defeat multiple cards in one turn only if it instantaneously defeats said card, if when continuously defeating cards, if a king hits a king all kings within a one card radius are set to one health, can move twice. all cards (unless specified) move once per turn ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Combos ace, 2, 3: placed in the healing spot if it is free of cards, all cards of the placer's suits are fully healed 4, 5, 6: places the king, queen, and jack of the placer's bonus suit, duplicates all cards of the placer's core suit 4, 5, 6, 7: removes fortification and strengthening of all of the enemy's cards in play 8, 9, 10: fortifies all of the placer's core suit cards in play, strengthens all of the placer's bonus suit cards in play jack, queen, king: all of the placer's cards in play can move and attack a second time 10, jack, queen, king: fortifies the placer's bonus cards and strengthens the player's core cards Joker, ace: defeats all 3 and 4 cards in play, fortifies all 2 and 5 cards in play Joker, 2: defeats all ace and jack cards in play, fortifies all queen and king cards in play ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Goal of the game The goal of War of the Suits is to defeat all of your opponent's core cards (if your opponent's core cards are all discarded but they still have bonus cards in play, the game goes on till all of their bonus cards are defeated. This gives them one last chance to win) ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Board setup The board will have a 3 x 6 layout At the start a joker is placed in the space closest to each player within the middle column The first 2 rows in front of each player is their territory no cards except their own can be placed there The healing spot cannot be attacked Cards (including the ace) can be forced out of the healing spot if there are no cards left in the players core card deck that can be placed If your deck has one joker card, ignore the joker board setup rule, the joker now goes in the middle of the board, use an outside method to decide who gets the card ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Important other things Fortify: allows a card to take a hit without losing any health Strengthen: doubles a cards damage Healing spot: An area for a core or bonus ace card, heals other cards that are in it (every turn, a harmed card heals 2 points of health) An unharmed card can be placed within the healing spot. Multiple cards can be in the healing spot. In the first 2 turns, no cards can be placed, only the joker cards can move, unless the deck only has one joker Combos can be placed in the healing spot ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Specialty Rules/Gamemodes Gamemode I - Shuffled War of the Suits 2 player Dealer vs. player Player: is given 5 cards to start, wins when they have all aces. After every turn, if they have cards remaining in their hand, they draw one card. If any of their cards died, they draw no cards Dealer: starts with 5 face down cards placed, place another five cards on top of the first five, wins if the player cannot draw anymore cards. Draws 2 cards at the start of their round. All cards of the dealer’s stays face down until one is attacked, then until the dealer’s next turn, it stays face up. Dealer cards stacked on top of another serves as fortification Gamemode II - Solo War of the Suits 1 player Player vs self It plays round by round Round one: four enemy cards, 3 face down, 1 face up. Draw 1 card to use, draw 3 other cards (these are not in active combat until your card dies, then draw one of them) Death cards are placed in a pile and retrieved at the end of every round Boss cards: Ace of hearts, Ace of spades, Ace of clubs, Ace of diamonds These rules are subjective, experiment! Now you can make the next rounds!
